# Zeyuan Guo (郭泽远)

Ph.D. student at **[Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT)](https://english.bupt.edu.cn/)**, formally advised by **[Prof. Chuan Shi](https://shichuan.org/)** and currently conducting research under the guidance of **[Enmao Diao](https://www.diaoenmao.com/zh-cn/)**, affiliated with **GAMMALab**.

## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at BUPT, where I previously received my bachelor's degree. My research broadly lies in machine learning, with current interests in graph machine learning, transformers, generative modeling, and graph foundation models.

Besides research, I also enjoy building small open-source tools that make everyday work or life more convenient :)

## Research Interests

- Graph machine learning
- Transformers and generative models
- Representation learning on structured data
- Graph foundation models
